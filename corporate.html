How it works

Three taps.
Real impact.

Designed for the office manager, not the sustainability team. Post leftovers in seconds — we handle everything else.

01

Snap a photo

After a catered event, open the app and take a photo of the leftovers. Our AI reads it and fills in the listing automatically — food type, quantity, everything. Zero typing.

02

We find a match

Our algorithm instantly matches your listing to a nearby nonprofit based on proximity, food preferences, and capacity. No manual searching.

03

Nonprofit picks up

The matched nonprofit gets a push notification and confirms pickup. You approve with one tap. Both sides confirm completion.

04

Get your ESG report

Every month we send a detailed impact report — lbs diverted, meals served, CO2 saved — ready to share with clients and partners.
