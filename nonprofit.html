How it works

Food finds you.
You just show up.

Register once, set your preferences, and get notified when food is available nearby. We match listings to your org automatically.

01

Register your org

Tell us about your organization — what food you can accept, how many people you serve, and your pickup radius. Takes five minutes.

02

Get matched

When a nearby corporate office posts surplus food that fits your preferences, you get a push notification instantly — before anyone else.

03

Confirm pickup

Accept the listing with one tap. You'll get the pickup address, window, and contact info. Coordinate directly with the office manager.

04

Log your impact

After pickup, log the meals received. Your impact history builds over time — useful for your own reporting and grant applications.
